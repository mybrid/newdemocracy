Overview: Old Democracy Comparison

Old Democracy here indicates any democratic government on Earth today and makes no distinction between things like parliamentary versus representative democracy.

New Democracy is a bit of a misnomer. Irreni Society is a social engineering project that includes every aspect of life and everyone on planet Earth. Whereas we tend to think of current democracies as Russian nesting dolls. Some city or local government that governs us is nested inside some larger land area government like a US State which itself is nested inside a national democracy.

Irreni Society is social engineering and as such has no government, let alone a nesting of governments.

So what’s with the New Democracy branding then? Well, if one asks someone what a democracy is then a common answer is one-person, one vote. Another is a government of the people, by the people, for the people. Irreni Society meets both of these definitions.

Historians have noted the US Constitution was an affirmation of what was more than an invention. For example, most Colonies already had jury trials. In addition, Colonies were made into States based on existing Colony formation being preserved without change where the biggest debate was slavery. In the same way Irreni Society is very much an affirmation. However, as an engineering project existing systems are analyzed, deconstructed and re-made with improvements.

For example, voting is still a principle currency in Irreni Society. One person, one vote is still a core principle. However, Irreni banks votes rather than cast votes. Banked votes are on deposit until withdrawn. Vote banks are a copy of today’s financial banks. Vote banks have walk-in support, ATMs and web sites to conduct currency transactions. However, major differences exist with financial banks. For starters, all votes are public. This eliminates polling. Vote banks can be tallied 24/7 to know exactly how people would vote. In today’s world a vote for President would be deposited and only changed for the next election. Vote banks also have security measures that are unavailable to vote casting today. For example, election day can happen randomly any day of the year as people always have votes on file.

Another example where Irreni engineering re-makes existing democratic principles is that of rights. Rights exist within Irreni Society. However, rights are changed to responsibilities. The key difference between rights and responsibilities is resource allocation. Irreni assumes only a limited amount of resources in the world, Responsibilities in Irreni engineering are all combined into a single pool of resources. Priorities of responsibilities are voted on by everyone as one plan to allocate resources against all responsibilities. Today, democracies assign rights to the resources of responsibility in isolation. It is better engineering to weigh total resources as one pool available for implementation of all responsibilities with priority. One can imagine a future pandemic where a large pool of world resources are required to move to meet the moment. Sacrifices in other responsibilities must be made.  In Irreni we all vote on what gets cut, including on things like shut downs.

Let’s dig a little bit more into the engineering of votes in Irreni engineering since voting is our primary exposure in Old Democracy.

In the US we cast votes directly for representatives at all levels of government. In addition many cities and States have ballot measurements. Some ballot measures are constitutional amendments where others are laws.

In Irreni votes are counted indirectly.

Irreni is a mesh society. Mesh is fundamentally different than Old Democracy in that the smallest government is the sovereign government and not the nation. Further there is only one sovereign government and not the Russian nesting doll design. That sovereign is the mesh or micro governing organization, MGO. MGOs are fixed in size. MGOs have exactly thirty people. As a sovereign unit then MGOs are the only government to have life-and-death power over members. MGOs are the only unit that can imprison, impart corporal punishment, and hold trials for MGO members. MGOs can issue their own currency, enact trade deals, and anything else a nation does today as sovereign.

If you are a US citizen then the good news is that your vote for President won’t be as meaningless as one vote among 340 million. Instead, your vote for President will be one vote of thirty votes. Your vote matters. The bad news is that your MGO is the only direct voting allowed. Votes for things outside the MGO are as the MGO itself. This is similar to how member nations of the United Nations all get one vote.

In summary the only direct vote one has in Irreni Society is for MGO related business. Business outside the MGO requires the MGO to vote as a unit. To better understand this design an example is helpful. Let’s say an MGO’s primary business is farming corn. Further, the MGO wants to distribute its surplus around the world to trade using various projects. I say projects and not MGOs because MGOs in Irreni design coordinate via projects. Project contracts are where MGOs trade. Say the farming MGO wants some smart phones. The MGO either amends an existing project contract with their trade or they initiate a new project contract. In this case the MGO then deposits votes via the vote bank for the projects they want to trade corn. The MGO voting as a block forces the members of the MGO to agree  on any MGO votes, perhaps by internally having a simple majority vote. MGOs only casting votes as blocks means MGOs are always represented as a block. Therefore personal direct voting only exists within the MGO.

Projects within Irreni engineering replace laws in current rule-of-law systems. Projects can nest to any level and this replaces local, State and Federal nesting of responsibilities.

Hopefully by now things are starting to come into focus for you. Irreni Society is New Democracy. It is of the people, by the people, and for the people. It is one person, one vote. It takes existing components of Old Democracy like voting and re-makes them like changing vote casting to vote banking. Projects replace rule-of-law and nesting governments.

Overview: Infinity And Beyond

To Infinity! And Beyond!

Irreni scales to infinity and beyond! Let’s dicuss!

I suppose I should give a sense of what is meant by the use of the word "scale". In computer science the world scale does mean growth. To say Irreni scales to infinity does mean Irreni does grow to infinity. However, the world scale also means "at every size". In other words that design scale works at size 1, 10, 100, 1,000 …​ A design that scales works at any size. In this chapter we are going to look at both size one and size infinity. Irreni scales to any size. Irreni can shrink as well as expand size.

Irreni scales infinitely and to any size big or small using one fundamental principle: all growth is horizontal.

Imagine there is a human society, the Spacies, that only exists on spaceships. Spacies have perfected building self-contained spaceships for human living. Ships have everything for those on board to live out a natural life. Power, food, water, medicine, dental, living quarters, and so on provide for life cradle to space grave. Further imagine that they realized the best way to survive is if all the ships had the same structural design. In other words these ships all supported the same population size.

A single ship design allows all the ships in the fleet to interchange parts. Manufacturing is simplified to one set of designs.

Let’s say the Spacies have a population of nine billion. The spaceship size is thirty people. This means there are 300 million ships. Thirty may seem too small. However the small size is intended to withstand disaster optimally.

Imagine a disaster occurs where some rogue asteroid tears through the fleet killing millions. Small ships have the best chance to change course at the last minute. Small ships minimize impact damage to approximate just the path the rogue asteroid takes. After the disaster all damaged ships have parts from millions of other ships to repair with or to use as lifeboats.

Now imagine the Spacies exist millions of years in the future. Over millions of years they have kept to the same perfected small design. The Spacies have colonized large portions of Milky Way. Imagine a fleet of Spacie ships meet that are hundreds of thousands of years old meet up with the latest versions. The spaceships are still the same size. The parts are still interchangeable. The fleet can expand and shrink seamlessly.

This is horizontal growth. Horizontal growth expands to fill the Universe or survives natural disasters the most. Horizontal growth expands in both time and space dimensions with equal success.

Now further imagine the Spacies have designed their spaceships to land on new planets and colonize them using the spaceship that flew through space. The spaceships land on solid ground and like a camper just roll out various ground features. Otherwise day-to-day life for the thirty people on the ship remains the same.

The Spacies have the most resilient and robust design for survival until the end of the Universe. Further, if we have any chance of stopping the expansion of the Universe technically in a trillion years or so then we’ll need to populate as much of the Universe as possible.

First lesson of infinite scale design is simplicity to the maximum extent possible is required. The simplicity provides resilience and robustness when disasters occur. Maximally complex designs scale the least.

One can intuitively understand that more complexity means more fragility by thinking about a game, Jenga. Jenga is a game where blocks of rectangular wood blocks are stacked. Blocks are removed until the structure collapses. One block removed collapses the entire structure. This is not possible with horizontal growth. Spread out all the blocks on the table. Remove one then only that block fails. All the others remain functional.

The US is going to completely collapse because it is complex. At some point one more Jenga block is going to be removed and the entire thing will collapse. Jenga in this case is corruption. The rich are robbing the US blind with a fever pitch. The rich are stealing everything that isn’t bolted down and they are loosening the bolts. They are playing Jenga in that they are all trying to remove blocks before the entire system collapses. This type of corruption is possible because the US society design is vertical and doesn’t scale.

When Irreni Society covers everyone on Earth there will be 300 million MGOs where each MGO houses thirty people. The rogue asteroid disaster design benefits still apply. Horizontal design is the most robust design and the maximal number of humans will survive a rogue asteroid. They just won’t be able to join projects unless their treatment of MGO members meet the responsibility requirements of treatment by project contracts. The project contract that contains the largest number of MGOs is called project zero.

Over time I expect that ever larger groups of MGOs will join project zero first since it has the least requirements and most MGO members. Then they will join more restrictive management of finer granularity ideology as they see fit.

Overview: The Personal Touch

The previous Overview chapters have talked about society at large, but what about the personal touch?

This is just an overview where the following Handbook chapters deal with the specifics of joining an MGO today. This chapter deals with a hypothetical situation that assumes Irreni MGOs already exist in 2026. It further assumes the entire world is MGOs so as to avoid any discussions about integrating with existing governments.

Is this going to get weird?

No. Yes. Maybe. Yes.

Previously I mentioned that Irreni is weird as a democracy because the society is designed to last indefinitely, until the end of the Universe. Today’s society lives in the moment where capitalism lives quarter-to-quarter and religion is always teeth gnashing that the end times are nigh.

So is it a valid question to wonder if MGO daily life will be weird? Like some hippy commune or some such?

No. Yes. Maybe. Yes.

As to the "No" answer, first realize Irreni Society is 100% voluntary and anyone or any MGO can opt out when things get too "weird". Once one joins then there is only one sovereign "nation" that has control over people, the MGO. MGOs have exactly thirty people. If the entire world today were Irreni Society there would be three-hundred million MGOs or "nations".

So the first anti-weird relief is that joining is voluntary and further millions of MGO lifestyles will exist to choose from.

The second anti-weird relief is MGOs are free to establish any governing lifestyle they choose. Someone can simply start an MGO with rules or a constitution of their choosing, including capitalism. MGOs can further create capitalist projects.

The third anti-weird relief is that MGO membership is contract driven and not "citizenship". Members sign up for MGOs. Contracts can last up to five years. In some respects this is analogous to sports today where players are recruited and sign contracts.

So that’s the "No" answer. If someone wants a lifestyle similar to what exists today before Irreni Society then reasonable approximations can be found.

As to the "Yes" answer the first thing is dependency responsibility.

Every MGO and therefore every MGO member is obligated to care for dependents. Care for children. Care for the elderly. Care for the mentally unhealthy. Care for the sick. No pawning off caring for dependents. In the US today we pawn off caring for our dependents to nursing homes, foster homes or just not at all leaving them homeless.

Irreni Society requires everyone to be treated with dignity. Everyone has responsibilities where responsibilities replace rights. Taking care of the least of us. Taking care of those who won’t and can’t take care of themselves. This responsibility will impact every person in every MGO.

Previously it was stated that Irreni Society initially only calls for people to work half-time, or twenty-hours a week. That’s because the other twenty hours is to be used taking care of dependents.

Irreni project zero has a sophisticated scheme for ensuring dependents are optimally taken care of. The objective is not fairness within MGOs. The objective is optimal well being of dependents. The discussion of this scheme is outside the scope of this book. What’s important here is to note that everyone in every MGO will be taking care of dependents of one kind or another. This is weird relative to today’s society although it shouldn’t be.

Ignoring dependents is a disgrace that condemns all capitalist societies today.

Next, new technology will also be weird. Mostly because it is just new.

Again the discussion of new technology is outside the scope of this book. This new technology is required for everyone one Earth. Here is a quick overview:

  1. Earth AI. There is one Earth AI so large it has sensors from every place on Earth as input. It’s primary purpose is to simulate all project proposals as a kind of environmental impact study for every project. Its secondary purpose is an Earth Virtual Reality.

  2. Earth Virtual Reality. This is part of the Earth AI project. MGOs will have camera rooms. The walls, ceiling and floor will have projections of a point on Earth. People appear in the simulation as they are in reality. The Earth virtual reality allows everyone on Earth to conduct business with anyone else. It will also follow space travels to the Moon, Mars and everywhere off Earth.

  3. MGO AI. Each MGO will have its own AI to enforce its rules. The rules the MGO chooses to live by are used as a data model for the AI to then give answers as to implementation of those rules. The MGO AI also acts as first level cybersecurity to defend against outside attacks.

  4. Vote banks. Votes deposited into the vote bank are always public. This is weird and runs counter to today’s private vote casting.

  5. Continuous monitoring. Everyone has a smartphone where they report everyday with health and living metrics. This continuous feed of health and living data enables project management to ensure responsibilities for treating MGO members with dignity and fulfill contract requirements.

Things will also be weird because MGOs are survival units. This will be like living on a spaceship like the starship Enterprise. There will be somewhat of a military-like aspect where emergency drills and what not will be scheduled regularly to ensure things work. The MGO members will respond to internal disasters such as fires and floods. That’s not to say outside help isn’t available. However, relying on outside help makes the MGO dependent and not a survival unit.

So, in short, things will be weird relative to today’s societies due to everyone having: 1. Dependency responsibilities, approximately twenty-hours per week. 2. New technology responsibilities 3. Survival unit responsibilities

So that’s the "Yes" Answer. Everyone will have new responsibilities that do not exist today. The closest comparison to these new responsiblities today is to civic duty and public service. Today our "civic" duty is paying taxes and voting. Arguably paying taxes is somewhat analogous to working so many hours per week for public service. However, voting is the least we can do. Irreni Society expects far more civic duty and public service starting with twenty-hours per week of caring for dependents.

As a side note, all of these new responsibilities are relative to today’s society. In the future, perhaps millions of years, we will have complete genetic engineering control. Dependency effort will then be reduced to near zero or some minimum. The long arc of history points towards desire and pure free will. However, natural disasters and other forms of chaotic events in the Universe will always create dependent responsibilities.

And the "Maybe" answer?

Weird is culture dependent. This is called culture shock. Initially Irreni Society is going to be a large culture shock. No nations, no cities, no governments, no capitalism, no currency, as well as many other components of today’s society no longer exist except within MGOs and possibly as limited projects. However, once Irreni Society becomes the norm over a few generations then weird today will be the normal tomorrow. So weird is always a matter of how much culture shock one is exposed to either by choice or by force.

Further, Irreni Society is an all volunteer society. Individuals can refuse to join. MGOs can choose to go rogue and reject all management.

The establishment of Irreni Society is not by force of revolution or overthrowing existing governments. Irreni Society is established by convincing people to join. Irreni is said to dissolve existing governments, not overthrow them. Irreni Society has no ideology itself, just MGOs and projects. This means MGOs can live side-by-side or inside existing governments as long as the ideologies of the MGOS and projects align and fit within that society.

So that’s the "Maybe" answer. Irreni Society is 100% voluntary and anyone or any MGO can opt out when things get too "weird".

The personal touch then is that things can be "normal" relative to today’s lifestyle in the sense that Irreni Society is 100% voluntary and one can simply just choose not to join. Once joining one can choose to create an MGO constitution or set of rules that approximate today’s lifestyle and keep things "normal" that way. Further, since there will be millions of MGOs, one can search for existing MGOs that have already established a lifestyle of your choosing. Find your tribe!

And "Yes"! Irreni Society is weird in that it creates a far more personal touch with millions of cultures relative to today’s hundreds of nations. Yes, civic and public service responsibilities in Irreni Society exist where everyone will incur on the order of twenty-hours per week of responsibility that far exceeds what is expected in today’s democracies. So Yes! Things will be weird.

Find your tribe!
